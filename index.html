I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. Prior to this I worked with Dr. Partha Pratim Talukdar at Machine and Langauge Learning (MALL) lab for nearly two years on Knowledge-aware Computer Vision. Prior to that, I worked under supervision of Prof. C. V. Jawahar and Dr. Karteek Alahari for my PhD dissertation on scene text understanding at IIIT Hyderabad. At IIIT, I was a reciptent of Microsoft Research India PhD fellowship 2012 and XRCI best doctoral dissertation award: first runner up 2015.

My current research interest spans Computer Vision and Knowledge Graphs. To be specific, I focus on using external knoweledge bases for addressing various Vision and Languange tasks.
